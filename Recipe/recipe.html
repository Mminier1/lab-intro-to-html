The Cheeseburger

Description

This Cheeseburger is my favorite sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 Hamburger buns
  • 1 Ground Beef Patty
  • 1 Cheese Slice
  • 1 Lettuce Leave
  • 1 Tomato Slice
  • 1 Onion Slice
  • 2 Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Cook the Beef Patty
  2. Toast the Hamburger Bun
  3. Assemble the Burger
  4. Serve and Enjoy
